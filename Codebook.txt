Variable name: DATEOFRECORDING
Note: The date on which the record was made. Format is <day><month><year>

Variable name: GDPRAWEST
Note: The raw - i.e. pre-check - estimation of GDP for the year in <year> as reported by the US Bureau of Economic Analysis. Figures are reported in billions

Variable name: POPULATIONINMILLION
Note: The estimate of population for <year>, as reported by the US Census Bureau. Figure is in millions

Variable name: UNEMPLOYMENTRATE
Note: the unemployment rate, as reported by the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Figure is in percentage.
